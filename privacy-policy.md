# Privacy Policy for Pill Pilot Go

**Effective Date:** July 21, 2026

Pill Pilot Go ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your information.

---

## Information We Collect

Pill Pilot Go stores the information you enter into the app, which may include:

- Medication names
- Dosages
- Medication schedules and reminder times
- Refill dates
- Dose history and adherence records

This information is used solely to provide the features and functionality of the App.

---

## How Your Information Is Stored

Your medication data is stored securely on your device.

If you choose to enable iCloud syncing (if available), your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account and is protected by Apple's security and privacy measures.

Pill Pilot Go does **not** sell your personal information.

---

## Notifications

Pill Pilot Go requests permission to send notifications so you can receive medication reminders and refill alerts.

You may disable notifications at any time through your device's Settings.

---

## Location Information

Travel Mode may request access to your device's location to automatically adjust medication reminders when you travel across time zones.

Location access is completely optional and is used only for this feature. You may disable location permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

---

## Third-Party Services

Pill Pilot Go does not sell, rent, or share your medication information with third parties.

If future versions of the App include optional services such as analytics or crash reporting, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

---

## Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to help protect the information stored within the App.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

---

## Children's Privacy

Pill Pilot Go is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal information through the App, they should contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

---

## Medical Disclaimer

Pill Pilot Go is intended to help users organize and manage their medication schedules.

The App is **not** a medical device and does **not** provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Medication reminders are provided for convenience only and should not replace the advice or instructions of a qualified healthcare professional.

Always follow the directions provided by your doctor, pharmacist, or other healthcare provider.

In the event of a medical emergency, contact your local emergency services immediately.

---

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated effective date will be revised accordingly.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the support information provided on the App Store listing.
